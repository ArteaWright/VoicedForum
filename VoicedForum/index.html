What We Are All About

Voice was created with community in mind. We work to partner with the community in addressing issues of policing and monitoring in low-income earning and urban areas of Charlotte, NC. This is a serious issue because it is often unknown that these areas experience a high rate of interactions with police officers. Which could result in these areas having more cases of unethical behavior being exuded by CMPD. Our goal is to provide a safe platform for community members, within the Charlotte area, to report wrongful treatment and unethical behavior they've experienced with CMPD. In addition, providing resources to help our users who have experienced any form of police violence.
